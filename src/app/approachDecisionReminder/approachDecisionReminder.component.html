Approach Decision and Bargain - Reminder

You will now choose which Proposer to approach and to bargain with.

Remember:

  • One Proposer completes this task alone and without any help

  • One Proposer has an AI-System to assist them in making a decision

  • One Proposer is substituted by the AI-System that makes a decision on their behalf. The Proposer still receives the potential payoff from this task, but the decision is made autonomously by the AI-System.


The AI-System (Machine-Learning) was trained using prior interactions of comparable bargaining situations and participant personality data.
